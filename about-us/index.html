About Us

Justin Johnson, Owner

Justin graduated Magna Cum Laude from Colorado State University with a Bachelors Degree in Information Technology/Cybersecurity, and started his IT career working for a local Atlanta retirement home as a IT Technician. Justin has obtained the “CompTIA Trifecta” (A+, Network+ and Security+) as well as ISC(2) Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP), and continues to work on higher certifications.

After working for the retirement home, Justin pursued other ventures into the Cybersecurity field, taking on IA roles for various government projects, where he continued to learn and develop his skills.

To keep up with the latest technologies, Justin runs a lab environment with several computers, continuously testing products for security, as well as learning how to fully utilize new software. During his free time, he can be found spending time with wife and family while watching his children grow up.
