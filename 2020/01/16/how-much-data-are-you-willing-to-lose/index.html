How much Data are you willing to lose?

Posted on January 16, 2020January 16, 2020 by Admin

Data Breaches aren’t just some Hollywood theatrical computer hacker thing that you only dream of. It’s the real deal, and the truth is, it really can happen to you. The worst part? If you aren’t taking a proactive approach to your Cybersecurity, you may fall victim to those who didn’t realize it happened until it’s too late.

In 2018, 56% of breaches took MONTHS, if not longer, to discover. That’s over half of the breaches not being recognized even within the first few weeks. Bigger companies have means and ways to detect these breaches, but the threat actors going against these companies also spend more time articulating how they will complete their attack.

Smaller to mid size businesses, and even consumers, oftentimes don’t have hardly any security measures in place like major enterprises do. Let that sink in for a second…

So why trust that you business is secure when no one has made it public that you have fell victim to a data breach. Why have the mindset of “it won’t happen to me” or “I don’t need any help from IT professionals”. Because if you aren’t securing your data, or trusting your data with the experts, how many months will it take you to discover a breach (if you even do).
