What’s my password again?

Posted on January 9, 2020January 9, 2020 by Admin

Do you find yourself constantly having to reset your password because you forgot it? What about needing that extra secure 14 character password with upper/lower, special characters and numbers? Tired of losing productivity because of waiting for that password reset email? Or for your system administrator to reset your password?

Password managers, when used correctly, can be that solution for not only keeping your passwords secure, but also saving you time. Three products I have used and recommend are SecureSafe (https://www.securesafe.com), LastPass (https://www.lastpass.com) and Dashlane (https://www.dashlane.com), but other solutions work just as well. (No we are not affiliated with any of these companies).

These password managers all offer both free tiers and paid tiers, along with business pricing. Some even offer secure storage on their cloud servers, although with limited data unless you pay.

When looking for password managers, be sure to look for one that offer at least 128-bit AES Encryption. Also look for one that can generate complex passwords for you as an added bonus. Be sure to read the reviews of any password managers you choose as well. Additional security features like 2FA (2 Factor Authentication) and other authentication methods (such as using a Yubi-Key) will provide one more layer of security.

And remember, your password manager is only as secure as the password you use to secure it. Don’t expect your password manager to be secure when you use “password123” to lock it.
