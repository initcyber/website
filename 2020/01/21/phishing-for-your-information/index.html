Phishing for your Information

Posted on January 21, 2020January 21, 2020 by Admin

Some of us know how to tell the difference between a legitimate e-mail and a fake/spam e-mail, but do your employees know?

Phishing e-mails are quick to get through the spam filters and often-times deceiving to most casual computer users. Having e-mails such as “ce0@yourcompny.com” or “John@micr5oft.com” can be deceiving depending on the e-mail application used. Does your company provide anti-malware solutions that include e-mail scanning for phishing attempts? Does your company also run “Phishing” campaigns to test your employees if they will open the e-mail and link inside of the e-mail?

At Init Cyber, we provide solutions for phishing attempts, and we can also run phishing campaigns at a random interval to test your employees (and even you). If your business is running without solutions in place to help mitigate phishing and other e-mail related attacks, how can you ensure your employees aren’t passing out your company credit card information, or log-in information to your bank accounts? What about passing out credentials for their own computers which can later lead to a breach?
