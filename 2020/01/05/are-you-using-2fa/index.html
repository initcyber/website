Are you using 2FA?

Posted on January 5, 2020January 7, 2020 by Admin

Are you using 2FA (Two Factor Authentication) for any of your online needs (e-mail, bank accounts, websites you visit with your sensitive information)? If you aren’t you really need to be. Even though hackers can possibly crack your password, having a TOTP/HOTP Two Factor Authentication Solution is that extra step that a hacker has to take to get into your information.

One tool I use for several different sites that support it is Authy, and their Desktop and Mobile solutions make 2FA easier.
