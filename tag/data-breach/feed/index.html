]]> When it comes to your company, how secure is your network? Do you have certain policies and practices in place that help mitigate cyber attacks and breeches? If you don’t have policies or any plans in place, the questions is… Why not? According to Forrester, 2/3 of the organizations out there have averaged five (5!!) or more breaches in the past two years.

Now you may be thinking “What? Not me, not my business” but do you truly understand what a data breech is? Anytime your information is accessed without prior authorization from you or your company is considered a breach. Often times this occurs and you may not even realize it. What can happen to your business if the data that is breached contained any PII (Personally Identifiable Information i.e. Social Security Numbers, etc) or your business inside secrets? Lets just say your bottom line just got a touch smaller…

So if you don’t have a dedicated IT staff or company assisting with your IT needs, and think that your data is secured without the help of a professional, then how small are you willing to let your bottom line go?
